# Vision & Strategy — Deep-Tech Pulse

## Why We're Building This
The team currently stays up to speed by talking to each other — but knowledge sharing breaks down when people are busy, out of sync, or working on different things. Important AI developments, tool updates, and workflow breakthroughs get lost in conversation. Deep-Tech Pulse exists to give the team a single, always-current place to see what's happening in agentic coding — without requiring anyone to curate it, post links, or maintain a wiki.

## Who We're Building For
**Primary users:** A small team of staff at the company who work with agentic coding tools (Cursor, Claude, Devin, etc.) daily. They need to stay current on AI developments that directly affect how they work — not general AI news, but specific agentic coding breakthroughs, tool updates, and workflow innovations.

**Secondary users:** None for v1. This is a team-internal tool. If successful, it could expand to the broader engineering organization.

## What Winning Looks Like (1 Months)
1. **Daily active usage** — every team member checks the dashboard at least once a day as part of their routine
2. **Default morning read** — Deep-Tech Pulse is the first thing someone opens when they want to know "what happened in AI today?"
3. **Zero maintenance** — the dashboard runs autonomously with no human intervention for content curation


## Strategic Bets
1. **AI summarization is good enough for news curation** — Claude Haiku (primary) with OpenAI fallback can reliably summarize, tag, and filter AI news with sufficient quality for a small team (validated via 50-article benchmark)
2. **Automated > curated for a small team** — fully automated ingestion with AI noise filtering produces a better signal-to-noise ratio than manual link sharing
3. **Zero login is the right tradeoff** — for a small team, the friction of authentication outweighs the benefit of per-user features.
4. **Urgency labels drive action** — tagging content as "Use Now", "Watch This Week", or "Coming Soon" helps the team prioritize what to pay attention to
5. **External signals proxy for relevance** — YouTube views, and AI relevance scores create a meaningful trending ranking even without click tracking
